Custom VFD Panel Solutions

Precision motor control engineered for your industrial needs

Overview

SyncAmpere's custom-engineered VFD panels integrate high-performance variable frequency drives with intelligent control systems to deliver precise motor speed and torque management across diverse industrial applications. Our solutions optimize energy consumption, improve process control, and extend equipment lifespan through advanced power electronics and intelligent algorithms.

Core Subsystems:

  • Power Conditioning: Advanced AC-DC conversion with power factor correction to improve input power quality and reduce harmonics.
  • Inverter Stage: DC-AC conversion with variable frequency and voltage output for single-phase or three-phase motor control.
  • Intelligent Control: Real-time microcontroller processing with V/f control, vector control, and field-oriented control algorithms.
  • Protection & Monitoring: Comprehensive system protection with current limiting, thermal management, and predictive diagnostics.
